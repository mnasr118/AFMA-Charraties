Our History

As the fire service adapts and changes, our Charities division continues to ​grow to serve our communities.. ​Starting in 2013 as Sun City West Firefighter Charities, our charities division has grown to include Sun Lakes, Tonopah, and Wittmann Charity foundations as one consolidated firefighter Charities board. ​ The AFMA Firefighters Charities offers assistance to members, citizens, and charitable organizations who have faced unforeseen ​circumstances with uncompensated financial assistance, perpetually maintaining our core values; service, integrity, and compassion.
